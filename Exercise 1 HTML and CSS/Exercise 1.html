Air Fryer Hot Chicken Tenders

These air fryer hot chicken tenders are crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, coated in a spicy seasoning blend. They are quick to prepare and make a flavorful meal or snack with minimal oil.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken tenders
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray

Method

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 200°C.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the egg and hot sauce together.
  3. Mix breadcrumbs with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Dip each chicken tender into the egg mixture, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray.
  6. Place tenders in a single layer in the basket.
  7. Cook for 10–12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with your preferred dipping sauce.
